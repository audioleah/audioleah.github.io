...to believers saddled by addiction and sin...

11 JUNE 2017

Loves Journey

...to believers saddled by addiction and sin... Come to the throne It is the guilty who need His mercy And the scheme is to keep them away But go on and come You who have gone astray Pick yourself up and come! Your heart cries out for help But you will find none in yourself And will find none in your guilt Nor in your depression or shame Yes come again, then again Please don't stay away Your penalty has been served No need to hide your faults. Standing before the throne where your Father seats, Lift up your hands and say, "You asked me to come" So just keep coming You'll be changed as you see Him Little by little The light will dispel the darkness His love will mend the pain As you see the Great Sacrifice Your life revealed in His death. The cross revealed at the throne Where your King still calls Himself "The Lamb" Slain for your sake forevermore Don't stay away, just come! .
